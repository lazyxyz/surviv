🎮 Welcome to SurvivFun

SurvivFun is a play-and-earn game built entirely on the blockchain.

  • ✅ All game items are NFTs stored securely on the blockchain.
  • ✅ All items are created directly on-chain for transparency.

🎁 How to Get Game Items

Open crates to get game items. Each crate requires one key to unlock.

🔑 How to Get Crates and Keys

You can get crates and keys in two ways:

  1. Buy from the Shop
    The quickest way to grab crates and keys is to buy them from the in-game shop.
  2. Earn in Play-and-Earn Seasons
    During Play-and-Earn seasons, you can earn crates, keys, and exclusive items by playing or competing:
    • Earn While Playing — Reach high ranks in matches to earn rewards:
      - Solo Mode: Top 5 ranks earn crates and keys.
      - Squad Mode: Top 3 ranks earn crates and keys.
    • Compete on the Leaderboard — Earn points to climb the leaderboard. Top players get big rewards at the end of the season.
💡 Winning isn’t easy, but don’t give up! Try squad mode to boost your chances of earning rewards!

🏆 How Points Work In Leaderboard

Solo Mode
1st Place: 10 points
2nd Place: 5 points
3rd Place: 3 points
4th Place: 2 points
5th Place: 1 point
Squad Mode
1st Place: 6 points
2nd Place: 3 points
3rd Place: 1 point
